These are some of the toy projects I've been playing with in my spare time for fun and learning

  • WafelLocator

    WafelLocator is an app designed to make it easy to locate all the Wafels & Dinges locations in New York and most importantly, to get the directions to the closest one!

    Source: https://github.com/linked82000/WafelLocator

  • Geocode

    Geocode is an app to plot an entered location on an overhead map as well as display a StreetView panorama. Think a watered-down, customized Google Maps. It's an amalgamation of Google Maps APIs, jQuery, and other things I wanted to play with!

    Source: https://github.com/linked82000/Geocode

  • GoDoAngular

    A mini "todo" list application done with AngularJS. A small and neat educational experience for me.

    Source: https://github.com/linked82000/GoDoAngular

  • GameOfLife

    A simple port of Conway's Game of Life.

    Source: https://github.com/linked82000/GameOfLifeJS

  • TicTacToe

    Super silly TicTacToe. Reminiscent of the earliest web sites circa the 1990s!

    Source: https://github.com/linked82000/TicTacToeJS
